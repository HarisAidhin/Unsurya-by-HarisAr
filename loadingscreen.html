Visi & Misi

Visi

"To become a Center of Excellence in higher education in aerospace and related fields, developed both nationally and internationally, with graduates who are ready to enter the workforce."

Misi

  • To provide excellent higher education in the field of aerospace and related disciplines, producing graduates who are independent, of strong character, professional, and ready to contribute in the workforce.
  • To serve as a higher education institution that acts as an agent of change in aerospace technology and related fields developed at UNSURYA.
  • To implement excellent, equitable, and sustainable higher education governance and management.
